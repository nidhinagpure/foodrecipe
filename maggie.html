RECIPE OF MAGGIE

How to Make Vegetable Masala Maggi

Step 1

Heat butter or oil in a pan. Add ginger-garlic paste, onion,
and saut√© till the onions are soft.

Step 2

Next, add tomatoes, mix vegetables,
and stir-fry on high flame till they are 90% cooked,
and they still have slight crispiness left.

Step 3

Then add water, maggi masala,
red chili powder, garam masala powder,
and mix well.

Step 4

Bring water to boil, add Maggi noodles,
close the lid, and cook for 2-3 minutes
or until water is absorbed.
Stir in between.

Step 5

Finally, sprinkle chaat masala and switch off the flame.

Step 6

Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve Maggi noodles recipe hot.
